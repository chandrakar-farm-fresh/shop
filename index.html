The Dhaan Ka Katora Heritage

Rooted in the rich, fertile soils of Chhattisgarh, our farm is part of a region historically revered as the Rice Bowl of India. We honor this legacy by rejecting mass-production shortcuts. Our focus is on the micro-details of farming—nurturing the soil, timing the harvest perfectly, and delivering grains that retain their natural aroma, texture, and nutritional integrity.

Curious About Our Harvest?

Supermarket grains often sit in massive storage silos for months or even years, losing natural moisture and flavor. We harvest, pack, and ship directly from our fields in Bhilai, ensuring peak freshness and maximum nutritional value.

We rely heavily on traditional, natural farming practices. We focus on soil health and natural pest deterrents to keep our crops safe and healthy without relying on harsh chemicals.

When you subscribe, we reserve a portion of our harvest specifically for your family. We will prepare and ship your box at the beginning of every month, ensuring your kitchen is never out of fresh staples.

The Dhaan Ka Katora Heritage

Rooted in the rich, fertile soils of Bhilai, Chhattisgarh, our farm is part of a region historically revered as the Rice Bowl of India. We honor this legacy by rejecting mass-production shortcuts. Our focus is on the micro-details of farming—nurturing the soil, timing the harvest perfectly, and delivering grains that retain their natural aroma, texture, and nutritional integrity.
